Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Whezy ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile game hub developed by Ogulcan. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Summary: Whezy does not collect personal information. All your game data stays on your device. We use analytics and ads through third-party services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. There is no account creation, login, email collection, or cloud storage of your data.

All of the following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers:

  • Profile name and avatar selection
  • Game progress, high scores, and achievements
  • In-game currency (gems) and purchased items
  • App settings (sound, haptics, theme preferences)
  • Voice recordings in VoiceZoo (stored temporarily on device, never uploaded)

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymized data:

Google AdMob - We display ads (rewarded, interstitial) to support the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. We request non-personalized ads by default. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics - We collect anonymized usage events (such as which games are played, scores achieved, and features used) to improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy.

Sentry - We use Sentry for crash reporting to identify and fix bugs. Crash reports may include device type and OS version but no personal information. See Sentry's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

Whezy displays ads through Google AdMob. You may choose to watch rewarded ads to earn in-game gems. Interstitial ads may appear between game sessions. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

On iOS, we will request your permission via the App Tracking Transparency framework before any tracking for ad personalization. You can deny this request and still use the app normally with non-personalized ads.

4. Microphone Access

The VoiceZoo game requires microphone access to record animal sounds as part of gameplay. These recordings are:

  • Stored temporarily on your device only
  • Never uploaded, shared, or sent anywhere
  • Used solely for the game's sound-matching feature

You can deny microphone access and still use all other games in the app.

5. Notifications

Whezy may send local notifications (daily game reminders, streak reminders). These are generated entirely on your device - no push notification server is involved. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Whezy is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we have no user accounts, no personal data is collected from any age group.

7. In-App Purchases

Whezy may offer optional premium subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information. All transactions are managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with anyone. The only data shared with third parties is anonymized analytics and crash data as described in Section 2.

You may choose to share your game score cards via the device's native share sheet. This is entirely user-initiated and optional.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your data. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
