Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between games?

Just swipe left or right in the game library to move between games. Tap a game to play it instantly — no menus, no loading.

How do I manage or cancel Whezy Premium?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions, select Whezy, and you can change or cancel your plan there. Your premium stays active until the end of the current billing period.

I already subscribed — how do I restore it on a new device?

Open the Premium screen in the app and tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Where is my game data stored?

All of your progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device. Whezy does not require an account and does not upload your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do ads work?

Whezy is free to play with ads. Watching an optional rewarded ad can earn you in-game bonuses. Whezy Premium removes ads entirely.

I found a bug or have a suggestion.

We'd love to hear from you — email whezygamehub@hotmail.com with a short description (and your device model if it's a bug) and we'll take a look.
